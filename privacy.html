ASP Manager ChatGPT Action Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

The private ASP Manager ChatGPT Action sends customer, job and quote details that the user supplies in ChatGPT into ASP Manager.

What is sent

This may include customer contact details, property address, job description, quote wording, prices, notes and photo links where available.

How it is used

This information is used only to create and manage customer, job and quote records in ASP Manager. It is not used for advertising, sold to third parties or made public by this integration.

Photos

If photo links are supplied, ASP Manager stores those links against the relevant job. ChatGPT attachments are not assumed to be permanent public links.

Security

The Action uses a private connection token. Credentials are not included in this policy or in the quote data.
